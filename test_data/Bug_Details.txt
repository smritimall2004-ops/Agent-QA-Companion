[Screen Readers-Service Now-Incident-Response Time]: JAWS is reading unavailable after expanding the "Show timing breakdown" button.

User Experience:
Users who relay on Assistive Technologies would be impacted if JAWS is reading unavailable after expanding the "Show timing breakdown" button.

Test Environment:
OS: Win 10 Version 1703
Browser: IE 11
URL: https://microsoftppe.service-now.com/navpage.do
Screen Readers: JAWS(18.0.4104)

Repro Steps:
Step 1: Open JAWS and Navigate to above URL 
Step 2: Navigate to "All applications" tab list through keyboard TAB and press Enter.
Step 3: Navigate to "Incident" tab through keyboard TAB and Press Enter.
Step 4: Navigate to "Create New" button through keyboard TAB and press Enter.
Step 5: Navigate to "Response Time" through keyboard TAB and press Enter to expand.
Step 6: Navigate to "Show timing breakdown" button through keyboard TAB and press Enter.
Step 7: Now verify JAWS behavior on this button after expanding.

Actual Result:
JAWS is reading unavailable after expanding the "Show timing breakdown" button.
JAWS is reading it as "unavailable expanded Show timing breakdown button unavailable expanded".
Refer attachments<MAS 4.2.1_ServiceNow_Response Time.png><MAS 4.2.1_ServiceNow_Response Time.wmv>

Expected Result:
JAWS should not read the unavailable after expanding the "Show timing breakdown" button.
JAWS should read it as "Show timing breakdown button expanded".

Issue repros throughout the application.

[Screen readers-ServiceNow-Order Something]: Incorrect role 'radiogroup' is defined for “yes” and “no” radio buttons which are present under the label 'FTE Sponsor(Are you an Employee?)'.

PWD Impact:
Visually challenged users who rely on Screen readers will be impacted if the incorrect role 'radiogroup' is implemented for the Yes and no radio buttons which are present under the label 'FTE Sponsor (Are you an Employee?)' in the page.

Test Environment:
OS : Windows 10 ,1909(OS Build 18363.418)
Browser: Chrome.(Version:78.0.3904.87), Edge Browser
Url: https://microsofttest.service-now.com/sp?id=sc_cat_item&sys_id=2c5ae6fddb48a7403f0156a5dc9619f0

Repro Steps:
Step 1: Open the above URL in Chrome/Edge browser with valid credentials.
Step 2: Order something page will be displayed.
Step 3: Navigate to “FTE Sponsor” radio button and select “yes” radio button.
Step 4: Press F12 and verify whether the role is defined correctly for the radio buttons present in the page or not.

Actual Result:
Incorrect role (Role=”Radiogroup”) is defined for “yes” and “No” radio buttons which are present under the label 'FTE Sponsor (Are you an Employee?)'

Note: Issue reproes to the similar radio buttons wherever appears in the page.

Expected Result:
Supported role should be defined for the 'Yes' and 'No' radio buttons which are present under the label 'FTE Sponsor (Are you an Employee?)'
